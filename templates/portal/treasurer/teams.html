How it works:
  1. A team registers publicly at the KYISA website (status = Pending)
  2. The county pays the one-time KSh {{ registration_fee|floatformat:0 }} county registration fee via M-Pesa or bank transfer
  3. You enter the M-Pesa/bank reference and confirm payment → Payment Confirmed
  4. You click Approve → team status becomes Registered
  5. A team manager login is automatically created using the team's contact email
  6. The temporary password is sent to the team manager email — they can then log in and add players
