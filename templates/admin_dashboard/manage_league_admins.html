User Management Guide

Available Roles

  • System Admin: Full system control
  • Organising Secretary: Manage competitions, fixtures & teams
  • Discipline Coordinator: Manage assigned discipline
  • Verification Officer: Verify county players
  • Referee: Match reports & appointments
  • Team Manager: Manage own team & players
  • County Sports Director: County registration & players
  • Treasurer: Verify payments & fees
  • Chair of the Jury: Handle appeals & hearings
  • Media Manager: News, articles & media
  • Secretary General: Oversight & audit
  • Scout: Talent scouting & shortlists

Actions Menu

  • View Profile: Full user details & activity
  • Change Role: Reassign user permissions
  • Toggle Status: Activate or deactivate account
  • Reset Password: Generate new one-time password
  • Delete: Permanently remove user
