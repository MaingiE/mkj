MKJ SUPA CUP - Governor Mutula Kilonzo Junior Supa Cup

Makueni County's premier youth multi-sport championship. Football, Volleyball, Basketball and Handball competitions across all six sub-counties of Makueni, Kenya. Featuring grassroots Ligi Mashinani ward leagues through to county finals. Live fixtures, results, standings and team registration at mkjsupacup.com.

MKJ SUPA CUP 2026 - 4th Edition. Organised by the County Government of Makueni. Open to youth aged 18 to 23 from Makueni, Kibwezi West, Kibwezi East, Kaiti, Kilome and Mbooni sub-counties.

  • Football Men - MKJ SUPA CUP Makueni County
  • Football Women - MKJ SUPA CUP Makueni County
  • Volleyball Men - MKJ SUPA CUP Makueni County
  • Volleyball Women - MKJ SUPA CUP Makueni County
  • Basketball 5x5 Men - MKJ SUPA CUP Makueni County
  • Basketball 5x5 Women - MKJ SUPA CUP Makueni County
  • Handball - MKJ SUPA CUP Makueni County

Governor Mutula Kilonzo Junior Supa Cup - Wote, Makueni County, Kenya. Contact: info@mkjsupacup.com
